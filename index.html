About CodeNewbie

CodeNewbie is a podcast for everyone in the process of learning code. The host Saron Yitbarek interviews someone from the CS community in every podcast (like Quincy Larson founder of FreeCodeCamp) and asks the questions that we all want to know. You can find the podcast on Podbean, Spotify and a lot more podcats streaming platforms.
